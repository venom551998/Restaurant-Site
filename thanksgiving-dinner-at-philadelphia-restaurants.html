Thanksgiving Dinner at Philadelphia Restaurants for 2018

Make Thanksgiving easy at one of these 25 local eateries...

1 of 1
1 of 1

This Thanksgiving, let one of Philadelphia’s awesome restaurants do dinner for you.

Dozens of great dining establishments offer sit-down meals for those aiming to avoid the cooking, baking, grocery-store rush and post-meal clean up that comes along with hosting Thanksgiving.

From elegant tablecloth and candlelit prix-fixe meals to down-home all-you-can-eats, here are our top picks for Turkey Day dinners in the Philadelphia region, arranged chronologically and alphabetically.

BONUS: Before dinner, head to the Benjamin Franklin Parkway for the annual 6abc/Dunkin’ Thanksgiving Day Parade, the oldest parade of its kind in the U.S. complete with festive floats, giant balloons, lively dancers, costumed characters, celebrities and marching bands.

01

P.J. Clarke's

November 21-23, 2018

P.J. Clarke’s Philadelphia in Washington Square celebrates its inaugural Thanksgiving with three days of special holiday offerings. On Wednesday, November 21, the menu includes bites like mini-cheeseburgers and buffalo chicken wings. Come Thanksgiving Day, diners enjoy roasted turkey with sage gravy, corn pudding, sweet potato pie and more. For Black Friday, the kitchen cooks up a brunch complete with dishes like pumpkin pie pancakes and smoked salmon omelets.

Where: P. J. Clarke’s, 601 Walnut Street

02

Bank & Bourbon

— Photo courtesy Loews Philadelphia Hotel
Thursday, November 22, 2018

Bank & Bourbon at Loews Philadelphia Hotel serves up a not-to-be-missed Thanksgiving buffet for $65 for adults and $22 for kids ages 12 and under. The spread includes fresh oysters and seafood; a carving station with roasted salmon, turkey and prime rib; and classic sides like brioche-sage stuffing, green bean almondine and candied yams. An array of pies in flavors like maple-bourbon, lemon meringue and cranberry are on the menu for dessert. Add an optional wine pairing for $35.

Where: Bank & Bourbon, 1200 Market Street

03

Chez Ben

Thursday, November 22, 2018

Want to enjoy a Thanksgiving dinner at a bistro inspired by Philly’s favorite Founding Father, Benjamin Franklin? Then head to Chez Ben for a $39-per-person meal with dishes like butternut squash soup, roasted turkey, mashed potatoes, green beans and butter pecan tarts. Add an optional wine pairing for $20 more.

Where: Chez Ben, 400 Chestnut Street

04

Chez Collette at Sofitel Philadelphia

Thursday, November 22, 2018

Chez Collette at Sofitel Philadelphia welcomes guests with an indulgent Thanksgiving buffet featuring goodies like grilled root vegetable gratin, garlic mashed potatoes, herb-brined turkey, prime rib and sweet potato mousse. Adults dine for $65, which includes a glass of champagne, and kids under 12 eat for $32.50.

Where: Chez Colette, Sofitel Philadelphia, 120 S. 17th Street

Davio's Northern Italian Steakhouse

Executive chef Chris Tavares prepares a crowd-pleasing Thanksgiving feast at Davio’s Northern Italian Steakhouse. Del Frisco's Double Eagle Steakhouse

Thursday, November 22, 2018

Enjoy three courses of Thanksgiving goodness at Del Frisco’s Double Eagle Steakhouse. Start the meal with a harvest salad or butternut squash soup before moving on to the main event: turkey with all the trimmings. For dessert, choose either pecan pie a la mode or pumpkin cheesecake. All of this deliciousness costs $59 per guest.

Where: Del Frisco's Double Eagle Steakhouse, 1426-1428 Chestnut Street

Devil's Alley Bar & Grill

— Photo courtesy Devil's Alley Bar & Grill
Thursday, November 22, 2018

Head to Devil’s Alley Bar & Grill for the annual Mama Devil’s Holiday Dinner. For $29.95 for adults and $19.95 for kids, guests enjoy a three-course meal complete with delicious options like roasted turkey, mac and cheese, sweet potato gratin and apple pie. Prefer to dine at home without having to cook? Take-out and delivery options are also available.

Where: Devil's Alley Bar & Grill, 1907 Chestnut Street

Devon Seafood Grill

Thursday, November 22, 2018

Let the team at Devon Seafood Grill do the work for you this Thanksgiving. From noon until 9 p.m., the Rittenhouse Square favorite serves a prix-fixe feast for $40 for adults and $20 for kids 12 and younger.

Where: Devon Seafood Grill, 225 S. 18th Street

Dim Sum House by Jane G's

— Photo courtesy Dim Sum House
Thursday, November 22, 2018

Celebrate Thanksgiving with an Asian twist at Dim Sum House by Jane G’s. Diners can order dim sum all day and enjoy the indulgent Norwegian whole king crab done three ways – steamed with ginger and scallions, with minced pork and XO sauce and salt-and-pepper style.

10

London Grill

Thursday, November 22, 2018

For an unforgettable Thanksgiving meal in Fairmount, diners flock to London Grill. This year’s traditional buffet includes options like squash soup, apple-cider-glazed ham, collard greens with bacon, cornbread-sausage stuffing and roasted turkey. The dessert menu features a selection of seasonal pies. Guests can partake in all of the dishes for $45 (kids 11 and younger eat for half price) or enjoy a limited menu for $25 at the bar.

Where: London Grill, 2301 Fairmount Avenue

11

McCormick & Schmick's Seafood & Steakhouse

Thursday, November 22, 2018

On Thanksgiving, McCormick & Schmick’s shifts its focus from steaks and seafood to (of course!) turkey. Alongside the bird, guests can enjoy all the fixings, like butternut squash soup, cornbread stuffing, apple-squash casserole, mashed potatoes and gravy, cranberry sauce and seasonal vegetables.

Where: McCormick & Schmick's Seafood & Steaks, 1 S. Broad Street

Moshulu

— Photo courtesy Moshulu
Thursday, November 22, 2018

For a truly unforgettable Thanksgiving, reserve a table at Moshulu, an elegant restaurant built inside of a historic Tall Ship. The three-course, $60 prix-fixe meal includes appetizers like tuna tartare and lobster bisque, entrees like pan-seared branzino and jumbo lump crab cakes and a dessert duo of pumpkin-spiced cheesecake and a chocolate-dipped pecan bar with cranberry-orange sauce.

Where: Moshulu, 401 S. Christopher Columbus Boulevard

Red Owl Tavern

Thursday, November 22, 2018

Give thanks while helping those in need at Red Owl Tavern. A special three-course holiday meal includes turkey with traditional sides and all the trimmings. A portion of each meal is donated to No Kid Hungry.

Ruth's Chris Steak House

Ruth's Chris Steakhouse — Photo courtesy Ruth's Chris Steak House
Thursday, November 22, 2018

In honor of the holiday, Ruth’s Chris Steak House offers a special three-course Thanksgiving feast. For $39.95, guests can enjoy a soup or salad starter, hand-carved turkey with sausage and herb stuffing, a side dish and a dessert duo. Diners who prefer to stick with red meat can order off of the restaurant’s regular menu.

Scarpetta

— Photo courtesy Scarpetta
Thursday, November 22, 2018

At The Rittenhouse’s elegant restaurant Scarpetta, guests can celebrate Thanksgiving in two ways: a la carte or with a $68 four-course, prix-fixe menu. Either way, the delectable menu includes options like creamy polenta, sweet potato gnocchi, pancetta-wrapped turkey and coconut panna cotta.

Where: Scarpetta, 210 W. Rittenhouse Square

16

Smokin' Betty's

— Photo courtesy Smokin' Betty's
Thursday, November 22, 2018

Midtown Village‘s popular Smokin’ Betty’s offers its very first Thanksgiving dinner this year. Diners can enjoy a three-course prix-fixe meal with holiday delights like roasted turkey, honey-bourbon baked ham, cornbread muffins and pumpkin pie. Dinner costs $29.95 for adults and $19.95 for kids. Although the kitchen closes at 8 p.m., guests can keep the party going at the bar until 10 p.m.

Where: Smokin' Betty's, 116 S. 11th Street

17

Spirit of Philadelphia

— Photo courtesy Spirit of Philadelphia
Thursday, November 22, 2018

Hop aboard the Spirit of Philadelphia for a Thanksgiving meal on a cruise along the Delaware River. The buffet spread includes options like bow tie pasta salad, green bean casserole, roasted turkey, mac and cheese and vanilla bean mousse. The two-hour trip departs at 3 p.m., and guests are asked to arrive by 2:30 p.m. Adult tickets cost $79.90, while kids cruise for $73.90.

Where: Spirit of Philadelphia, 401 S. Columbus Boulevard

Square 1682

Thursday, November 22, 2018

Don’t pass up a chance to help those in need while enjoying a sumptuous Thanksgiving dinner at Square 1682. The Rittenhouse Square eatery serves up a special prix-fixe meal alongside holiday cocktails. A portion of all sales are donated to No Kid Hungry.

Where: Square 1682, 121 S. 17th Street

The Bercy

— Photo courtesy The Bercy
Thursday, November 22, 2018

For Thanksgiving on the Main Line, head to The Bercy in Ardmore. Diners can choose a-la-carte dishes or opt for a three-course prix-fixe menu for $55 that includes crowd-pleasers like pumpkin soup, Lancaster turkey with gravy, braised beef short rib and apple tarts. Turkey trays for kids cost $15.

Where: The Bercy, 7 Lancaster Avenue, Ardmore

The Farmhouse & Bistro at People’s Light

Thursday, November 22, 2018

Looking for a Thanksgiving feast in Malvern? Reserve a table at The Farmhouse & Bistro at People’s Light. For $45 for adults and $20 for kids under 12, guests enjoy favorites like herb-roasted turkey, pan-seared pink trout and bourbon mashed sweet potatoes with candied marshmallow.

Where: The Farmhouse & Bistro, 39 Conestoga Road, Malvern

21

The Radnor Hotel

Thursday, November 22, 2018

Give thanks this year at The Radnor Hotel, where the expansive holiday buffet costs $39.95 for adults and $17.50 for kids ages 5-11. The menu spans a variety of options, including imported and domestic cheeses, roasted turkey with giblet gravy, candied yams with marshmallows, cranberry sauce and cauliflower au gratin. Be sure to save room for sweet treats at the popular chocolate fountain.

Where: The Radnor Hotel, 591 Lancaster Avenue, Wayne

22

The Twisted Tail

Thursday, November 22, 2018

Head to Society Hill to celebrate Thanksgiving at The Twisted Tail. For $49 for adults and $25 for kids under 10, diners get to choose from entrees including roasted Lancaster turkey, herb-crusted prime rib and pineapple-glazed ham. Sides like cornbread stuffing, baked beans and mashed potatoes are served family style, and the meal ends with apple, pecan or pumpkin pie.

Where: The Twisted Tail, 509 S. 2nd Street

XIX (Nineteen)

Thursday, November 22, 2018

The holiday buffet at XIX (Nineteen), the stunning restaurant at The Bellevue Hotel, is a meal for which to be thankful. Guests can stuff themselves silly with options like a raw bar, an assortment of salads, cheese and charcuterie, coffee-and-molasses-brined turkey, oyster chowder, cookies, cupcakes and more.

Where: XIX Nineteen, The Bellevue Hotel, 200 S. Broad Street

Philadelphia’s modern steakhouse Urban Farmer at The Logan serves up a Thanksgiving feast that is sure to please the whole family. Chima Steakhouse

November 22-23, 2018

Meat, meat and more meat is always the draw at Chima Steakhouse, but for Thanksgiving, the menu also includes specialties like roasted turkey, stuffing, cranberry sauce, mashed sweet potatoes, green bean casserole and cornbread. All of this holiday goodness comes with a price tag of $56.90 per person.
